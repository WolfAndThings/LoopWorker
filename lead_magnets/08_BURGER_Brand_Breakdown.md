## BRAND ATTRIBUTES

### Personality
- Unapologetically indulgent
- Bold, loud, confident
- Quality-focused but not pretentious
- Modern American diner energy

### Visual Language
- Bold typography, impactful
- Red, black, cream color palette
- Raw, messy, appetizing
- Foil textures, paper, casual packaging
- Flash photography, harsh shadows

### Photography Style
- Direct flash, harsh shadows
- Slightly chaotic, messy
- Close-up details (cheese pull, meat texture)
- Real, not overly styled
- Paper/foil packaging visible
