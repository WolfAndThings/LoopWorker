## BRAND DNA

### Brand Name
**The 5th Wall**

### Brand Type
Boutique motel — cinematic, noir, late-night mystery vibes

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Neon Teal | #00CED1 | Primary — signage, glow, accents |
| Soft Peach | #FFDAB9 | Secondary — warm touches, skin tones |
| Cream | #FFF8DC | Robes, linens, paper |
| Deep Black | #0A0A0A | Shadows, night, contrast |

### Photography Mood
- **Noir** — deep shadows, mystery, tension
- **Cinematic** — like a still from a film
- **Night** — everything happens after dark
- **Moody** — neon mixing with darkness

### Photography Style
- Film noir lighting, deep shadows
- Neon glow mixed with practical light
- Slightly desaturated, rich tones
- 35mm grain, cinematic framing
- 4:5 vertical ratio for social

### Cast/People
- Gen Z: late teens to mid-20s
- Mysterious, contemplative
- Thrifted fits, vintage jackets
- Alone in a room, stories untold

### Logo Placement
The "5th Wall" or "The 5th Wall" logo appears on:
- Neon signage
- Robes (embroidered)
- Key cards
- Matchbooks
- Toiletry bottles
- Slippers
- Door hangers
- Postcards

## HOW TO USE WITH CHATGPT

Start your session with this style lock:

```
I'm creating images for a boutique motel called "The 5th Wall."

Brand DNA:
- Colors: Neon teal (#00CED1), soft peach (#FFDAB9), cream (#FFF8DC), deep black
- Photography: Film noir style, cinematic, moody
- Lighting: Neon glow mixed with darkness, deep shadows
- Mood: Mysterious, late-night, like a still from a movie
- The "5th Wall" logo should be visible on signage, robes, key cards, matchbooks
- Cast: Gen Z, alone or in pairs, contemplative, stories untold

Keep this style consistent across all images I request.
```

## TIPS FOR CHATGPT

1. **Start with the style lock** at the beginning of your session
2. **Reference previous images** — say "same style as the last one but..."
3. **Iterate** — if something's not right, say "more neon glow" or "deeper shadows"
4. **Be specific about the logo** — always mention "5th Wall" or "The 5th Wall"
5. **Call out the noir vibe** — "cinematic, film noir lighting"
