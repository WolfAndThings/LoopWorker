# The 5th Wall — 20 Prompts
## Teal, Peach, Cream. Gen Z Energy. Every Shot Branded.

---

## STYLE LOCK

```
For all images: On-camera flash, harsh shadows, 35mm film grain, raw and gritty. Vertical 4:5 frame. The 5th Wall branding visible in every shot — logo on signage, robes, key cards, matchbooks, amenities. Teal, peach, cream brand colors. Gen Z cast — late teens to mid-20s, thrifted fits, experimental style.
```
