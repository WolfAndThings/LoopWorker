## 1. THE HERO LOOKBOOK SHOT

```
Female model mid-20s standing against white brick wall. Wearing oversized camel wool coat, white t-shirt underneath, dark straight-leg jeans, white leather sneakers. Hands in coat pockets, relaxed stance, looking off-camera left. Natural daylight from large window to the right.

Camera: Full body, straight-on, model fills 70% of frame
Style: Editorial minimalist, clean shadows, natural skin
```

```
Camera: Macro detail, fabric fills entire frame
Style: Tactile, premium, you can almost feel it
```
