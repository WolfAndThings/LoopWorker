## BRAND DNA

### Brand Name
**BCBC** (Bike Club Bike Club)

### Brand Type
Urban cycling collective — community rides, social cycling, city exploration

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Electric Yellow | #F7E733 | Primary — logo, accents, spoke cards |
| Black | #0D0D0D | Text, jerseys, bikes |
| White | #FFFFFF | Backgrounds, contrast |
| Asphalt Grey | #4A4A4A | Secondary, urban context |

### Photography Mood
- **Urban** — city streets, concrete, grit
- **Movement** — bikes in motion, dynamic
- **Community** — group rides, collective energy
- **Early morning** — golden hour, empty streets, the city before it wakes

### Photography Style
- Documentary street photography
- Motion blur on wheels, sharp on riders
- Harsh morning light, long shadows
- Slight grain, high contrast
- 4:5 vertical ratio for social

### Cast/People
- Mixed ages, 20s-40s
- Diverse, urban, athletic
- Cycling kit mixed with street clothes
- Serious about riding, not about fashion

### Logo Placement
The "BCBC" logo appears on:
- Jerseys and bibs
- Cycling caps
- Spoke cards
- Water bottles
- Musette bags
- Stickers
- Banners at meetup spots

## TIPS FOR CHATGPT

1. **Start with the style lock** at the beginning of your session
2. **Reference previous images** — say "same style as the last one but..."
3. **Iterate** — if something's not right, say "make the logo more visible" or "add more motion blur"
4. **Be specific about the logo** — always mention "BCBC" by name
5. **Call out colors** — "electric yellow, not gold or mustard"
