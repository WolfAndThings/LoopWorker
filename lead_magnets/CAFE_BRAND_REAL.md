## THE 10 ASSETS

### 1. GOOGLE BUSINESS HERO
**Where it goes:** Google Maps listing, first photo
**Why it matters:** This is the first thing people see when they search for you

```
Interior of specialty coffee shop, morning golden hour light streaming through large front windows. Empty of customers, clean and inviting. Warm wood tones, minimal decor, espresso machine visible behind counter. Wide angle showing the full space.

Style: iPhone photo quality, natural and authentic, not overproduced. Warm but realistic lighting.

Avoid: Stock photo look, too perfect, artificial lighting, HDR effect
```

---

### 2. INSTAGRAM GRID — SIGNATURE DRINK
**Where it goes:** Instagram feed, pinned post
**Why it matters:** Shows what you're known for

```
Overhead shot of latte with leaf art in ceramic cup on wooden table. Morning light from window creating soft shadow. Partial view of pastry on small plate, napkin edge visible.

Style: Natural phone photography, warm tones, casual but intentional composition

Avoid: Perfect symmetry, studio lighting, stock photo aesthetic
```

---

### 3. WEBSITE HOMEPAGE HERO
**Where it goes:** Top of website, full-width
**Why it matters:** Sets the entire brand tone

```
Barista hands pouring steamed milk into ceramic cup, mid-pour, latte art forming. Shot from customer's perspective across the counter. Warm café interior soft in background, morning light.

Style: 35mm film aesthetic, warm golden tones, shallow depth of field, slightly grainy

Avoid: Sterile, corporate, overly sharp, stock photography
```

---

### 4. YELP/TRIPADVISOR — FOOD SHOT
**Where it goes:** Review site listings
**Why it matters:** Makes people hungry

```
Fresh croissant on small ceramic plate next to cappuccino, morning light from window. Crumbs visible on worn wooden table. Authentic, just-served moment.

Style: Natural photography, foodie but not fussy, warm inviting tones

Avoid: Staged, perfect plating, studio food photography look
```

---

### 5. ONLINE STORE — PRODUCT SHOT
**Where it goes:** E-commerce, bean sales page
**Why it matters:** Sells your retail product

```
Single kraft paper coffee bag on marble surface, whole beans scattered around base. Soft window light from left creating gentle shadow. Simple, minimal, premium feel.

Details: Readable but not prominent label area, natural imperfections in beans, warm tones

Style: Clean product photography with warmth, not sterile

Avoid: Floating product, pure white background, Amazon listing style
```

---

### 6. INSTAGRAM STORY — BEHIND THE SCENES
**Where it goes:** Daily stories
**Why it matters:** Shows the human side

```
Close-up of hands pulling espresso shot, coffee dripping into cup. Chrome portafilter, steam rising. Shot from barista's POV looking down.

Style: Vertical format (9:16), phone camera quality, authentic behind-counter moment

Avoid: Too polished, landscape orientation, staged
```

---

### 7. EMAIL HEADER — SEASONAL PROMO
**Where it goes:** Newsletter, seasonal campaign
**Why it matters:** Drives seasonal sales

```
Iced coffee in clear glass with condensation, summer afternoon light. Fresh mint sprig, orange slice. Bright, refreshing, seasonal energy.

Style: Warm but bright, summery, inviting, slight lifestyle context

Avoid: Winter vibes, dark moody, stock photo
```

---

### 8. ABOUT PAGE — TEAM PHOTO
**Where it goes:** Website about section
**Why it matters:** Builds trust and connection

```
Candid portrait of barista in apron, genuine smile, leaning against counter. Natural window light, coffee shop interior soft in background. Authentic, not posed.

Style: Documentary portrait, warm tones, real skin texture, shallow depth of field

Avoid: Corporate headshot, perfect lighting, stock photo smile, over-retouched skin
```

---

### 9. MENU BOARD — CATEGORY HEADERS
**Where it goes:** In-store digital menu, printed menu
**Why it matters:** Organizes and sells menu items

```
Artistic close-up of espresso with perfect crema, ceramic cup edge visible. Dramatic side lighting creating depth. Rich brown tones.

Style: Slightly editorial, warm, appetizing, works as background for text overlay

Avoid: Busy composition, distracting elements, flat lighting
```

---

### 10. SOCIAL PROOF — CUSTOMER MOMENT
**Where it goes:** Social media, testimonial section
**Why it matters:** Shows real people enjoying your space

```
Over-the-shoulder shot of person reading book at café window seat, coffee cup in hand. Afternoon light, cozy atmosphere. Face not visible — about the moment, not the person.

Style: Lifestyle photography, warm tones, candid feeling, aspirational but achievable

Avoid: Model-looking person, staged pose, looking at camera
```

## HOW TO USE THESE

### In ChatGPT:
1. Copy the prompt
2. Generate the image
3. If you like it, use the next prompt with: "Generate in the same style as the previous image"

### Building Your Brand:
- Start with #3 (Website Hero) — this sets your look
- Generate #1-10 maintaining that style
- You now have a complete brand photo set

### Making It Yours:
Swap these details to match your actual shop:
- "Ceramic cup" → your actual cup style
- "Kraft paper bag" → your packaging
- "Worn wooden table" → your actual surfaces
- "Morning light" → your shop's actual lighting situation
