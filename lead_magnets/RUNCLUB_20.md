# Run Club — 20 ChatGPT Prompts
## A Complete AI Brand Asset Pack

---

## BRAND DNA

### Brand Name
**Run Club** (or customize: "[City] Run Club", "The Run Club", etc.)

### Brand Type
Urban running collective — group runs, community, the ritual of morning miles

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| Neon Coral | #FF6B6B | Primary — logo, accents, laces |
| Off-White | #FAF9F6 | Tees, tanks, backgrounds |
| Black | #1A1A1A | Text, shorts, contrast |
| Concrete Grey | #6B6B6B | Urban context, secondary |

### Photography Mood
- **Raw** — sweat, effort, real bodies
- **Dawn** — the city at 6am, empty streets
- **Community** — running together, not alone
- **Grit** — harsh flash, visible texture, not polished

### Photography Style
- Documentary/street photography
- Harsh flash mixed with dawn light
- Visible sweat, real skin texture
- Motion blur on legs, sharp on faces
- 4:5 vertical ratio for social

### Cast/People
- Mixed ages, mixed paces
- Real runner bodies (not fashion models)
- Diverse, inclusive
- Serious about miles, light on ego

### Logo Placement
The "Run Club" logo appears on:
- Tees and tanks (front chest, back)
- Running caps and headbands
- Water bottles
- Race bibs
- Banners at meetup spots
- Stickers

## HOW TO USE WITH CHATGPT

Start your session with this style lock:

```
I'm creating images for a running group called "Run Club."

Brand DNA:
- Colors: Neon coral (#FF6B6B), off-white (#FAF9F6), black (#1A1A1A)
- Photography: Documentary/street style, harsh flash, raw and real
- Lighting: Dawn light mixed with flash, harsh shadows
- Mood: Community, effort, the ritual of morning miles
- The "Run Club" logo should be visible on tees, tanks, caps, bottles
- Cast: Mixed ages, real runner bodies, diverse, inclusive

Keep this style consistent across all images I request.
```

## TIPS FOR CHATGPT

1. **Start with the style lock** at the beginning of your session
2. **Reference previous images** — say "same style as the last one but..."
3. **Iterate** — if something's not right, say "more sweat" or "add flash shadows"
4. **Be specific about the logo** — always mention "Run Club" by name
5. **Call out colors** — "neon coral, not pink or red"
6. **Customize the name** — replace "Run Club" with your actual club name
