# THE PROMPT FRAMEWORK
## Stop Writing Wish Lists. Start Art Directing.

---

## THE PROBLEM

Most people prompt like this:
> "A beautiful product photo of a perfume bottle with good lighting"

And get: Generic. Flat. Stock photo energy.

**This guide fixes that.**

---

## THE A.R.T.S. FRAMEWORK

Every killer prompt has 4 components:

### A - ATMOSPHERE
The emotional quality of the scene.
- What feeling should this evoke?
- What's the energy?
- What time of day/season/moment?

**Keywords:**
- Nostalgic, intimate, warm
- Bold, energetic, urgent
- Serene, minimal, calm
- Raw, gritty, honest
- Luxurious, elevated, refined

### R - RENDERING
How should this be captured/created?
- Photography style
- Lighting setup
- Color treatment
- Film stock/digital quality

**Keywords:**
- 35mm film grain, Kodak Portra
- Editorial flash photography
- Soft diffused natural light
- High contrast, crushed blacks
- Warm/cool color grade

### T - TANGIBLES
What's physically in the frame?
- Subject (with specific details)
- Environment/setting
- Props and context
- Spatial relationships

**Keywords:**
- Hands (weathered, manicured, etc.)
- Specific materials (ceramic, brass, linen)
- Environmental details (scratched wood, steam rising)
- Position/action (cradling, pouring, resting)

### S - SPECIFICATIONS
Technical camera/render details.
- Lens and focal length
- Depth of field
- Aspect ratio
- Any specific rendering notes

**Keywords:**
- Shot on 50mm lens
- Shallow depth of field
- 3:4 aspect ratio
- Macro detail
- Wide establishing shot

## BEFORE & AFTER EXAMPLES

### Example 1: Coffee Product

**BEFORE (Wish List):**
> "A beautiful photo of a coffee bag on a table"

**AFTER (A.R.T.S.):**
> **A:** Early morning ritual warmth, quiet contemplation
> **R:** 35mm film aesthetic, warm window light from left, visible grain
> **T:** Kraft paper coffee bag with hand-stamped logo, resting on worn butcher block, steam rising from nearby cup, scattered coffee beans
> **S:** Shot on 85mm, shallow depth of field, 4:5 ratio

**Combined Prompt:**
> "Early morning ritual warmth, quiet contemplation. 35mm film aesthetic, warm window light from left, visible grain. Kraft paper coffee bag with hand-stamped logo resting on worn butcher block counter, steam rising from nearby ceramic cup, scattered whole coffee beans catching light. Shot on 85mm lens, shallow depth of field, 4:5 aspect ratio."

---

### Example 2: Skincare Product

**BEFORE (Wish List):**
> "Professional product photo of a face cream jar"

**AFTER (A.R.T.S.):**
> **A:** Serene spa moment, elevated self-care
> **R:** Soft diffused light, clean minimal aesthetic, slight warmth
> **T:** Frosted glass jar with minimalist label, resting on pale marble, single eucalyptus sprig, water droplets on surface
> **S:** Product hero shot, 100mm macro, 1:1 ratio

**Combined Prompt:**
> "Serene spa moment, elevated self-care ritual. Soft diffused light from above, clean minimal aesthetic with slight warmth. Frosted glass skincare jar with minimalist sans-serif label, resting on pale veined marble slab, single eucalyptus sprig casting soft shadow, delicate water droplets on glass surface. Product hero shot, 100mm macro lens, 1:1 square aspect ratio."

---

### Example 3: Fashion Editorial

**BEFORE (Wish List):**
> "Cool fashion photo of someone in streetwear"

**AFTER (A.R.T.S.):**
> **A:** Raw urban energy, confident rebellion
> **R:** Flash photography, harsh shadows, high contrast black and white
> **T:** Model mid-stride, oversized vintage denim jacket, empty parking structure, motion blur in extremities
> **S:** Wide shot, 35mm lens, dramatic angle from below, 16:9 ratio

**Combined Prompt:**
> "Raw urban energy, confident rebellion. Direct flash photography creating harsh shadows, high contrast black and white treatment. Young model mid-stride wearing oversized vintage denim jacket, empty concrete parking structure as backdrop, subtle motion blur in hands and jacket edges suggesting movement. Wide shot from below using 35mm lens, dramatic upward angle, 16:9 cinematic ratio."

## FRAMEWORK CHEAT SHEET

Copy and fill in:

```
ATMOSPHERE: [emotion] energy, [mood] moment

RENDERING: [photography style], [lighting], [color treatment]

TANGIBLES: [subject with details], [environment], [props/context]

SPECIFICATIONS: [lens], [depth of field], [ratio]
```

---

## COMMON ATMOSPHERE WORDS

| Mood | Words to Use |
|------|--------------|
| Warm | Nostalgic, intimate, cozy, golden, familiar |
| Cool | Serene, minimal, clean, sophisticated, calm |
| Bold | Energetic, confident, dynamic, powerful, raw |
| Luxe | Elevated, refined, opulent, precious, rich |
| Gritty | Raw, authentic, honest, textured, real |

---

## COMMON RENDERING WORDS

| Style | Words to Use |
|-------|--------------|
| Film | 35mm grain, Kodak Portra, analog warmth, soft focus |
| Editorial | Flash, harsh shadows, high contrast, magazine |
| Natural | Window light, soft diffused, golden hour, organic |
| Studio | Controlled lighting, clean backdrop, product hero |
| Cinematic | Anamorphic, color graded, shallow DOF, widescreen |

---

## COMMON TANGIBLE DETAILS

| Element | Specific Words |
|---------|----------------|
| Hands | Weathered, manicured, calloused, elegant, working |
| Surfaces | Scratched, worn, patina, marble, raw wood, concrete |
| Textures | Grain, weave, matte, glossy, textured, smooth |
| Environment | Steam rising, dust particles, morning light, shadow play |
| Imperfections | Asymmetric, slightly off-center, natural variation |

---

## ACTION STEPS

1. **Before your next prompt,** write out each A.R.T.S. component separately
2. **Combine them** into one flowing description
3. **Generate 5-10 images**
4. **Note what worked** and refine
5. **Build a template** from your wins
