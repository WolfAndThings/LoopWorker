# Lucky Strike — 20 Prompts

## STYLE LOCK

```
For all images: On-camera flash, harsh shadows, 35mm film grain, raw and gritty. Vertical 4:5 frame. Lucky Strike branding visible in every shot — logo on signage, shoes, cups, scoring screens, uniforms. Red, gold, cream brand colors. Gen Z cast — late teens to mid-20s, thrifted fits, experimental style.
```
