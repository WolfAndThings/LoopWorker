# 10 Coffee Shop Shots
## Specific Prompts. Real Scenes. Actual Assets.

---

## 1. THE BARISTA AT WORK

```
Male barista in worn canvas apron, mid-30s, focused expression. Standing at La Marzocco espresso machine, tamping ground coffee into portafilter. Fresh grounds visible on counter, steam wand in background. Morning window light from left.

Camera: Shot from customer side of counter, waist-up framing
Style: Documentary, 35mm film warmth, natural skin texture
```

---

## 2. THE POUR

```
Close-up of hands pouring steamed milk into white ceramic cup. Latte art leaf pattern forming. Milk pitcher chrome catching light. Espresso crema visible beneath milk. Counter surface shows coffee drips and used tamper.

Camera: Overhead angle, tight on cup and hands only
Style: Warm tones, shallow depth of field, slight motion blur on pour
```

---

## 3. THE GRIND

```
Whole coffee beans falling from hopper into commercial grinder. Beans suspended mid-air, some landing in dosing chamber. Ground coffee piling up. Barista hand on grinder lever.

Camera: Close-up, side angle catching light through beans
Style: Action freeze, warm golden backlight, coffee dust visible in air
```

---

## 4. THE FINISHED DRINK

```
Cappuccino in thick-walled ceramic cup on small saucer. Rosetta latte art, slight imperfection showing it's hand-poured. Wooden counter with visible grain. Single small spoon on saucer. Morning light creating soft shadow.

Camera: 45-degree angle, cup fills 60% of frame
Style: Warm, inviting, cafe table POV, phone-photo natural
```

---

## 5. THE COUNTER SCENE

```
Espresso machine mid-shot with two portafilters locked in. One shot pulling — espresso streaming into cup. Knock box with used grounds visible. Stack of ceramic cups. Male barista's torso and hands visible working. Menu board soft in background.

Camera: Wide shot of working station, eye level
Style: Documentary, busy but not chaotic, warm morning light
```

---

## 6. THE BEANS

```
Burlap sack of whole coffee beans, top folded open. Beans spilling onto worn wooden surface. Small metal scoop resting in beans. Partial origin stamp visible on sack — "Ethiopia Yirgacheffe."

Camera: Overhead, full sack in frame, rustic surface texture
Style: Natural light, raw ingredients feel, earthy warm tones
```

## 7. THE PASTRY CASE

```
Glass pastry case showing croissants, muffins, and scones on ceramic plates. Handwritten price tags. Warm morning light reflecting off glass. Coffee cup on counter beside case.

Camera: Customer POV looking into case, slight reflection
Style: Appetizing, warm, authentic bakery feel
```

## 8. THE CUSTOMER MOMENT

```
Woman's hands (rings visible) wrapped around warm ceramic latte cup at wooden table by window. Laptop edge visible. Afternoon light. Face not in frame — just hands and cup.

## 9. THE BAG ON SHELF

```
Three kraft paper coffee bags on wooden retail shelf. Each bag different roast level — labels reading "Light," "Medium," "Dark." Small chalkboard price signs. Warm ambient lighting.

Camera: Straight-on product shot, retail display context

```

## 10. THE END OF DAY

```
Empty espresso machine, portafilters removed and resting on drain tray. Clean knock box. Stack of clean cups. Sunset light through window. Closed sign visible in background reflection.

Camera: Wide shot of cleaned station, golden hour light
Style: Quiet, satisfying, day's work done

## HOW TO USE

### Step 1: Pick Your Hero
Run prompt #1 or #5 first — these set the overall look.

### Step 2: Lock the Style
Tell ChatGPT: "For all following images, match this exact visual style."

### Step 3: Build the Set
Run prompts 2-10. Each one adds to your brand library.

### Step 4: Use Them
- #1, #5 → Website, About page
- #2, #3, #4 → Instagram feed
- #6, #7, #9 → Product pages
- #8 → Social lifestyle content
- #10 → Story content, closing time post
